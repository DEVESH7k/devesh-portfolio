My Approach

DevOps Philosophy

"DevOps is not a tool. It's not a team. It's a culture of shared responsibility — where developers own security, operations own quality, and everyone owns the pipeline."

— Devesh Khatik
01
🔄
Automate Everything

If you're doing it manually more than twice, automate it. From infrastructure provisioning with Terraform to container scanning with Trivy — every repetitive task is a pipeline opportunity.

CI/CDIaCGitOps
02
🔐
Shift Security Left

Security isn't a final gate — it's baked into every commit. SAST with SonarQube, CVE scanning with Trivy, IAM least privilege, secret scanning — all integrated pre-production, not post-incident.

SASTTrivySonarQube
03
📊
Observe Everything

You can't fix what you can't see. Prometheus metrics, Grafana dashboards, structured logging, and on-call discipline mean we catch problems before customers do — every time.
