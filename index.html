About Me

I'm Devesh Khatik, a DevSecOps Engineer at ProTechmanize based in Mumbai. I specialize in building and securing cloud-native pipelines across AWS and Azure — with a strong focus on shift-left security.

Before DevSecOps, I worked at HERE Technologies as a Data Specialist, where I managed location datasets and cloud infrastructure on AWS. That transition taught me to see infrastructure holistically — from data pipelines to deployment pipelines.

I write about real-world DevOps & DevSecOps on LinkedIn, Medium, and Hashnode — sharing what I learn from managing 156+ CI/CD pipelines in production daily.
